Our reputation

Vitality Capital has a strong reputation in the UK property finance market for the consistency of our approach and the quality of our service, regardless of prevailing market conditions.

Over recent years whilst other lenders have withdrawn from the market, or restricted their lending criteria, we have continued to provide property finance in our core markets. In this way we have both supported long established clients and developed new relationships with developers and investors disenchanted with the inconsistent approach of many of our peers.

Our expertise

Our expertise extends across the property market, with a particular focus on providing finance for:

  • Residential development
  • Pre-let commercial development
  • The acquisition of tenanted commercial investment property with asset management opportunities

Where we are

We are active throughout England and Wales, and operate from offices in the City of London and North West. The North West is the original home of our business.

What we offer

Our loans generally range from £150,000 to £20,000,000 for development facilities, although larger facilities may be considered on a case by case basis.

We have a relationship, rather than transactional, approach to lending. We develop a strong understanding of our clients' business, and tailor our facilities accordingly.

Recent transactions

This website explains our lending philosophy and sets out key criteria for our core activities. It also provides examples of recent transactions we have funded, showing how we have assisted clients since the onset of the Credit Crunch. We hope that these examples give you a feel for our approach and show our commitment to the UK property market at a time when others have been absent or less than wholehearted participants.
