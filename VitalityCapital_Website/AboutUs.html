About Us

Our business is run by a team of highly experienced lenders with a wealth of property experience.

This enables us to provide an exceptional service characterised by:

  • Speed of response
  • Detailed knowledge of the sectors we finance as well planning and development processes
  • Flexibility to meet our borrowers' needs
  • A consistent and reliable approach

Our expertise

Our expertise extends across the property market, with a particular focus on providing finance for:

  • Residential development
  • Pre-let commercial development
  • The acquisition of tenanted commercial investment property with asset management opportunities

Each loan application is considered on its own merits and loan facilities are structured and priced to suit the nature and requirements of the specific case.

Our minimum loans generally start at £150,000, unless there is the strong prospect of additional business. Our maximum for individual development and investment loans is £20,000,000, although we are able to lend more by exception. It is not unusual for us to be funding a number of schemes for a client at any one time.

All loans are granted for a period that is appropriate for the transaction, with repayment usually from property sales or refinance.

We are comfortable lending to established companies as well as special purpose vehicles that have been incorporated specifically for the purpose of the transaction. When funding a development, particular emphasis is placed on the project management team employed to deliver it, so when lending to a company, we usually require personal guarantees, although we are prepared to limit these as appropriate. Primary security generally consists of a mortgage debenture over the borrowing vehicle and a first legal charge over the property being funded.
